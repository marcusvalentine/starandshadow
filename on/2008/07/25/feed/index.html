Star And Shadow Events 2008-07-25 to 2008-07-25https://www.starandshadow.org.uk/on/2008/07/25/feed/Star And Shadow Events 2008-07-25 to 2008-07-25en-gbSat, 15 Sep 2018 21:50:02 -0000MADE IN USSRhttps://www.starandshadow.org.uk/on/festival/104A three day event celebrating USSR culture will take place on Friday July 25 from 7:30pm at the Star and Shadow Cinema in Newcastle upon Tyne. Visitors will not only be able to feast on a range of typical Russian food, including some of the country’s delicacies, a Soviet dance troupe will provide entertainment while others can view an exhibition of Soviet old-timers: a real life Moskvich 408, a car made in USSR in 1966 that stars in the film 'Mrs. Ratcliffe's Revolution', and the legendary ‘Ushastik’ – big eared Zaporojets ZAZ 968, a car made in USSR in 1971.
