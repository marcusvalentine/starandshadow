This thirty-two minute Video-DVD was shot on location in Barbados, Guyana and Trinidad. It examines current intra-regional migration with a focus on the Guyanese migrant experience into Barbados. It explores the views of the host country, exposes institutional abuse & demonstrates gaps between the official stand and the actual experience of unskilled Caribbean migrants. An exclusive Q&A via Skype with the director/ artist, Annalee Davis (based in Barbados) will follow the screening!
