Anti-Valentines Weekend

Friday 13 to Saturday 14 February 2015

Be happy on the most miserable day of the year with friends, lovers and random third parties.

There’s Film and Live Music, Beer, Cocktails, Wine, just have a cup of tea with friends, dress-up, dress-down, be yourself, have a laugh. Come Feel the Love…

Friday 13th February 2015 @ 7:30pm

Film: “Radical Friends”:An inspiring female. environmental activist, on a mission to make friends with whom one can change the world, asks us to pledge our love of Mother Earth. Skype discussion with the director after the film, Free entry but: Donations to the Newcastle Community Green Festival.

Saturday 14th February 2015  @ 2:00 to 5:00pm.

Event: “Queer Speedmating”:Don’t see enough Queers around town? Neither do we! Want to get to know who your local queers and peers are? Us too! An afternoon of 2-minute meetings to make mates.

Saturday 14h February 2015 @ 5:30 to 7:30pm

Film: “Some Like It Hot”:The all-time outragaeous, satirical, comedy farce favourite. One of the most hilarious, raucous films ever made. Price: £4.00. Stay for Music afterwards £6.00

Saturday 14th February 2015 @ 8:30pm to 1am.

GIG: “Ladies of Midnight Blue”:

are an Afro-Latin percussion and brass duet. Afterwards DJ’s will span genres, dancing until 1am. Price: £5. Film+Music £6.00
