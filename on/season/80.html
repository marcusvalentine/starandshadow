Sunday Silvers

Sunday 2 January to Sunday 18 December 2011

The Sunday Silvers hopes to return you to the decadence of cinema!

Ever have that feeling that you'd just like to pop along to the flicks and see an old Hollywood movie that maybe you know but maybe you have never seen, or maybe you have seen it and you love it so much that you want to see it again on the big screen?

Well, the first Sunday of every month will bring you a celluloid Hollywood 'classic'.

+ Ushers!

The idea of this season is to make cinema-going special again and to bring back some of that romance.

The Sunday Silvers hopes to return you to the decadence of cinema! What with the digitalisation of cinema, we want to give film a chance! As it becomes harder and harder to see real film on the big screen, and as television begins to show classic titles during the day and less in the evening, here at the Star and Shadow we are KEEPING IT REEL!

Doors open at 7pm and the films start at 7.30pm on the dot.

+ NEWS JUST IN…

Cinema organist, Maestro Carl Heslop, will be playing the old organ before each screening. Get there early, sit back and enjoy what Sunday Silvers is all about.
