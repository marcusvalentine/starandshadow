Event: Canny Little Library

1 May 2011, 3 p.m.

The canniest collection of books at home in the Star and Shadow Cinema. Focussing on non-fiction with a critical stance towards our society, including local and international zines and historical pamphlets. Great books! Categories including; queer, diy, politics, history, auto/ biography, feminism, cookery, poetry and more, yes really more! The library is open for all for browsing and borrowing for free. Also available: access to the Star and Shadow permanent collection of film and theatre books.
