Event: A History Of Sound Art

6 Feb 2011, 2:30 p.m.

A piece of sound art that plots the history of sound art: an insightful retrospective into the craft of sound and its development as an artistic practice, from Edison's first sound film in 1895 to today. A History of Sound Art coincides with WET SOUNDS UK TOUR 2011, which is held at Newcastle's City Pool on the 5th and 6th February.
