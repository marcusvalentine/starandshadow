09

Event: Participate-Orama!

9 Jul 2011, noon

The Star and Shadow Cinema is run collectively by volunteers, using consensus. Consensus is widely regarded as one of the most empowering ways of making decisions in a group. At best, it allows people to come together as equals and find creative win:win solutions that everyone is happy with. However, it's not always easy! The Star & Shadow and Seeds for Change present 2 workshops on consesnsus decision making and facilitation. Whatever experience of consensus you have had - good, bad, or non-existent, you are very welcome to come along and join in with these two workshops.
