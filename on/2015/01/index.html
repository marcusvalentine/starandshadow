Film: Dancer In The Dark (2000) + Yeah You Gig (2000)

17 Jan 2015, 7:30 p.m.

Dancer in the Dark is a 2000 Danish musical drama film directed by Lars von Trier and starring Icelandic singer Björk, Catherine Deneuve, David Morse, Cara Seymour, Peter Stormare, Siobhan Fallon Hogan, and Joel Grey. The soundtrack for the film, released as the album Selmasongs, was written mainly by Björk, but a number of songs featured contributions from Mark Bell and the lyrics were by von Trier and Sjón. Three songs from Rodgers and Hammerstein's The Sound of Music were also used in the film. The film will be followed by a performance from Newcastle based Yeah You! - an Edgy Electronics duo consisting of Ekasilikon (voice) & MYKL JAXN (beats) http://www.wegottickets.com/event/303983
