Gig: R. Stevie Moore (Nashville) W/ Silver Fox, St James Infirmary & D J Richard Dawson

4 Jun 2012, 7 p.m.

The Godfather of home recording returns to Geordie shores! Following on from his much loved Newcastle, sell out show at the Star & Shadow last August, R. STEVIE MOORE returns once again w/ his band Tropical Ooze in tow and with a brand spanking new double album, recorded with long term friend/disciple ARIEL PINK.Born in 1952 to famed ELVIS + ROY ORBISON Bass player BOB MOORE, since 1966 - R. STEVIE has recorded nearly 2,000 songs on over 400 very original homemade albums of alarmingly idiosyncratic variety and styles and is often considered a seminal pioneer in the DIY & home recordists ethic.Support comes from Newcastle's own punk pop sweethearts SILVER FOX & ST JAMES INFIRMARY w/ a special DJ set from RICHARD DAWSON to boot!All this and MOORE on Monday 4th June from 7pm. Advance tickets are available online or over the counter from Beatdown Records, Reflex & RPM while stocks last!Records.http://www.wegottickets.com/event/158241https://www.facebook.com/events/394342077260064/
