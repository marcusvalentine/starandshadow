A Little Lost

10:00 p.m. Saturday 5 to 4:00 a.m. Sunday 6 December 2009

Following the hula hoop crazed night back in July, and after providing the post band entertainment for the equally popular Arthur Russell night, A Little Lost return to the Star and Shadow cinema…

The next night will feature the talents of:

Pete Morris (A Little Lost), who played two sets of sublime disco to sell-out dancefloors at the recent Arthur Russell night, and first A Little Lost (check out his mix on the link below),

The lynx-on-the-prowl sounds of Pat (Road to Rimini),

The Newcastle debut of the disco deviance of Jack and John from Glasgow,

The jaunty post-punk tones of DJ Pete,

And Oscar and Kelly joining up the dots…

All in the ethos of A Little Lost, which prioritises playfulness and fun above all else!

DECOR AND OUTRAGEOUS DANCING

If you came to the first two nights you’ll remember the colourful décor: shiny silver drapes hanging from the ceiling, hula hoops, ribbons… And we have more surprises for this one! So for a night of outrageously shameless dancing, ranging from camp to the sublime, come and enjoy yourselves in one of the friendliest nights in Newcastle, in what is undoubtedly the city’s friendliest venue.
