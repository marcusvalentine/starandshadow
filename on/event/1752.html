Public Outreach at Ouseburn Community Centre

2:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. Sunday 19 July 2015

Here is a description of the activities taking place on Saturday & Sunday
2pm Open: Chat to us over tea, coffee and homemade cake. This will continue through out the whole day parallel with the Activities below;
2pm-6pm: Drawing on Film workshop by Debbie & Rachel. using blank reels of film, you can draw your own film.  We will screen it at the end of the day.
2.30pm Free Film Screening: The Secret of Kells. Animated Adventure for all the family
3.45pm Animation Workshop: Arto will show you how to make an animated film. All materials will be provided.
5.00pm A series of film shorts made by film artists of the Star& Shadow
6.00pm Phallictite - A Performance by a non-traditional, improvised music ensemble. Skills or musical instruments are not necessary!
6.30pm A free screening of a documentary film yet to be decided.
Other activities may be added

Public Consultation and Community Outreach
Star and Shadow Cinema is excited to be relocating from Stepney Bank to 210 Warwick Street, NE6.
Please join us for an afternoon and evening of free films, refreshments and chat to find out what we are about. We will show you our architects plans, our volunteers will answer any questions you may have and we will welcome your comments and feedback.
When: Saturday July 18th 2pm – 8pm and Sunday July 19th 2pm – 8pm
Where: Ouseburn Community Centre, NE6 5PA
A little bit about us…
We are a not-for-profit co-operative entirely volunteer run. We
show inspirational and educational films not found in mainstream cinemas.
We provide facilities for film makers, other artists and community
groups. We also have a quiet bar, cafe and a venue
space where musicians and artists of various genre often perform.
We will be able to provide even more community facilities and artistic events in our
new building where people of all ages, abilities and backgrounds are welcome and encouraged to get involved.
We look forward to seeing you at the consultation. Please refer to our website and Ouseburn Community Centre for the line-up of films and workshop to be shown on both days.
