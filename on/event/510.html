Eddie & Sheena

8:30 p.m. Saturday 24 to 1:00 a.m. Sunday 25 September 2011

DIRTY ROCK & ROLL and FILTHY PUNK ROCK

Some people like punk AND rock n roll; they like garage AND girl groups; they like the New York Dolls AND Gene Vincent;  Suicide AND Little Richard.

That’s why we’re playing them all at EDDIE & SHEENA.

It’s a gathering of the booted, greased, leathered and t-shirted. For the kind of  wastrels who shout ‘play some fuckin’ Stooges’ at the wedding DJ.

Punks, Teds, ‘billies, deviants and everything in between.

If you own the Sun Sessions AND Nuggets, love Brian Setzer AND Joey Ramone, are Eddie OR Sheena, then come on down and meet the folks.

Your DJs: Michael Clunkie Vinyl Junkie and Mink Jaguar.

Entry £3
