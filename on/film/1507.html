Calamity Jane

Part of the Meet Me At The Star And Shadow

7:30 p.m. Wednesday 20 August 2014

Director: David Butler
Certificate: BBFC U
Length: 1hr 41 min
Format: Unknown
Language: English
Country: USA

Released in 1953. Very loosely based on the life of Wild West heroine Calamity Jane the musical explores an alleged romance between Calamity Jane and Wild Bill Hickok in the American Old West. The film starred Doris Day as the title character and Howard Keel as Hickok. It won the Academy Award for Best Original Song "Secret Love".

Here's your chance to come and recreate a little slice of Deadwood in your favourite movie theatre…

The score, with music by Sammy Fain and lyrics by Paul Francis Webster, includes:

  • "Calamity Jane: The Myth, The Woman, The Legend"
  • "The Deadwood Stage (Whip-Crack-Away!)"
  • "I Can Do Without You"
  • "It's Harry I'm Planning to Marry"
  • "Just Blew in from the Windy City"
  • "Hive Full of Honey"
  • "My Heart Is Higher Than a Hawk (Deeper Than a Well)"
  • "A Woman's Touch"
  • "The Black Hills of Dakota"
  • "Secret Love", which topped the Billboard and Cash Box musical charts at number one.
