Underwire - Women Filmmakers Night

Part of the NO SEASON

7:30 p.m. Sunday 10 March 2013

Director: Unknown
Certificate: Unknown
Length: Unknown
Format: Unknown
Language: Unknown
Country: Unknown

Are you a woman who makes films? Would you like to show them? Then come along!

This is an open film night for women who make films and who would like to show their work. Just come to show a film, to watch films, or to chat!

If you would like to show a film on the night, either just come down with you film, or you can let us know in advance by sending us an email to info@starandshadow.org.uk

If you don't want to show a film, but you're working on films and would like to meet like-minded people, just come!

If you don't want to show a film, and don't make films, but just want to come to see what the other films are like, you're also welcome!

Are you a man or a woman who would like to watch films? Then come along!

The night is in NO WAY forbidden to men - of course they can attend. Any man or woman can attend this event, whether they makes films or not.
We will only show films made by women though, as they only make a small minority of the filmmakers worldwide (about 5% to 10%), and that we would like to encourage them!

This is a FREE event, for everyone!
