¡VAMOS! Festival Film Season: Revolución + After Party with The Funky Butt Club (2010)

Part of the ¡VAMOS! Festival 2011: Film Season

7:30 p.m. Saturday 25 June 2011

Director: Various
Certificate: Unknown
Length: 100 mins
Format: Unknown
Language: Spanish (English subtitles)
Country:

Ten directors at the forefront of Mexican cinema, including Gael García Bernal, join together to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the Mexican revolution in 10 short films.

Screening supported by the Hatton Gallery as part of Revolution On Paper exhibition & the Mexican Embassy in London

+ After Party with The Funky Butt Club goes Latin after the film! (from about 9.30pm)

Funky Butt Club goes Latin playing upbeat retro dance tunes from the Caribbean, latino New York and Latin America!

The stunningly beautiful and moving film explores the uneasy relationships formed between the indigenous Guarani peoples and landowners in Mato Grosso Do Sul, Southern Brazil. Followed by a discussion with Fiona Watson, Campaigns Director Survival International & Jared Bowers.
