World Mental Health Day (Film + Gig)

Part of the Gloaming Elephants

6:30 p.m. Thursday 10 October 2013

Director: Ian Breakwell
Certificate: Not Rated
Length: 40 mins / 14 mins
Format: 16mm
Language: English
Country: UK

A short film by Ian Breakwell and Kevin Coyne followed by an 

evening of musical performance from the members of NAGAS (Newcastle And Gateshead Art Studio) 

in recognition of World Mental Health Day 2013.

.

LINE UP:

6:30pm -The Institution(1978) 50 mins, Ian Breakwell, UKStaring musician Kevin Coyne The Institution develops the questioning of 'normal' viewing habits, of harrowing documentaries and social conscious dramas presented to us on the nations small screens.

Clip: http://luxonline.org.uk/video/artists/ian_breakwell/the_institution.html

7.30: Refreshments and mingling in Bar area, etc

8.00: Live Music session

Sean McDermott (acoustic guitar)

Selma Star (ukulele)

Matt Coyle (Laptop and rap)

NAGAS Music group with Ziad (full band)

The Purrs -Jacki Sams and co (full band)

9.45: Karaoke 

11.30: Finish
