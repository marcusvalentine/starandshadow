Independent Film Center Vorky Team Of Ruma Presents

Part of the Kino Climates - Independent Cinemas Of Europe

7:30 p.m. Thursday 24 July 2014

Director: Various
Certificate: Unknown
Length: 90mins
Format: Various
Language: Unknown
Country: Serbia, ex-Yugoslavia
Vorky Team was established to commemorate Serbo-American film artist Slavko Vorkapich, in Ruma, Serbia, 2005, the county where he was born. The Vorky Team brings together artists and filmmakers, they organize screenings and debates, but also produce films. This year they started an International Festival of One Minute Film Mister Vorky.

Films with short talks in between.

 - The Life and Death of 9413: a Hollywood Extra (1928), Slavko Vorkapich and Robert Florey, 14:00mins, short experimental

 - Mister Vorky, International One-Minute Film Festival (2014), animation festival clip + 17 best one minute films

 - The Morning (2006), Dragan Cakic and Milan Milosavljevic, 18:00 mins, short

Program curator and presentation: Milena Popovic and Milan Milosavljevic

http://vorkyteam.blogspot.co.uk/

http://mistervorky.blogspot.co.uk/
