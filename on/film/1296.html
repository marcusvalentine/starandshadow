Estamira

Part of the Gloaming Elephants

6:30 p.m. Wednesday 11 December 2013

Director: Marcus Prados
Certificate: Unknown
Length: 121mins
Format: DVD
Language: Portuguese
Country: Brazil

This screening has been re scheduled from the original cancelled date: Wednesday 2nd October

as part of the Mental Health Film Season Gloaming Elephants.

“My mission, besides being Estamira, is to reveal the truth”

In the periphery of Rio de Janeiro, the 63 yr old Estamira is a happy and charismatic woman that has been working for more than twenty years in the city dump. Schizophrenic, but very charismatic, she’s the leader of a small community of old people with a very lyrical and philosophical attitude towards life.
