Press Play Festival: EJECT PARTY

8:00 p.m. Saturday 2 to 3:00 a.m. Sunday 3 October 2010

Feast your eyes and ears at the Press Play EJECT Party…

Featuring PRMNT VIBES dj's, a WARP FILMS showcase thern Vicki Bennett aka People Like Us will perform a live A/V concert editing and collaging found footage where you will be taken on a magical journey delving into the past which in turn provides perspective into our present. 

                                                                      

Next up 7” Cinema have curated us a very special selction of visual and audio treats…and to round the night off a gig from Pitchfork favourite C Powers who flexes J. Dilla-inspired 80s glitz onto his own lo-fi template of hip-hop and dance music, skirting the outskirts of floaty Mount Kimbie-style dub with a wonky synth-drenched wooze.

Do or D.I.Y.
