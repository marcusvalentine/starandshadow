Richard Dawson album launch with Cath & Phil Tyler and Nev Clay

8:00 p.m. to 11:00 p.m. Wednesday 5 October 2011

In Richard's own words:To celebrate the release of The Magic Bridge we'll be having a right old knees-up on the 5th October. I'm really looking forward to playing a nice long set, much from the new album and quite a few older tunes… reckon I might get dressed up for the occasion too…and I'm very glad to tell you Cath & Phil Tyler will be performing too! They play traditional folk songs with a verve, warmth and authenticity that is unmatched… Cath quite simply has one of the most startling voices you'll hear and Phil's guitar and banjo playing is just plain brilliant. I love them.NOW! with added Nev Clay! The great man will be performing a set of poetry, no music, just his incredible words… prepare to be stunned! Ephemeral rays of delight - funny, beautiful, sad and life-affirming. There was no way this night could happen without my lovely friend!Maybe there'll be someone else, maybe there won't… still deciding, yo!Doors 7:30, first act 8:155 quid.
