Francois and the Atlas Mountains

8:00 p.m. Friday 20 to 12:00 a.m. Saturday 21 November 2009

ABOUT FRANCOIS…

"François arrived in the UK from Bordeaux, France in Autumn 2003. Armed with a guitar, Kaos pad and a self released album and spurred on by a frustration with the lack of inspiration or like minded souls in his homeland, he immediately threw himself into the Bristol music scene." François is a musician, filmmaker, painter and more. He has toured the UK, Europe and North America extensively, frequently collaborating with members of Electralane, Camera Obscura and the like.

He will be playing tonight with his band, Francois And the Atlas Mountains.
