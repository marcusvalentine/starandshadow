John Maus + Plug

8:00 p.m. to 11:00 p.m. Monday 28 March 2011

John Maus makes some absolutely amazing music, incredibly romantic yet funny and intelligent at the same time, kind of sounding like you're watching an 80s teen romantic fantasy, Electric Dreams perhaps. You’re the clean-shaven guy with slicked back strawberry blond hair and a tight-fitting red jacket, or the girl with a black perm, and your love for each-other is so pure in a ridiculously romantic 80s teen flick kind of way it just it hurts. Oh, and there’s some villain standing between you.

Anyway, John Maus is the soundtrack to your sad and melancholy fantasy, and his live show is spectacular. A one man war machine going berserk on stage whilst playing with his drum machines, sequencers, keyboards and what not. The man's an F-ing genius and he simply hasn't received the recognition he deserves. On top of this he's a philosophy lecturer in Hawaii(!!).Have we mentioned he also played with Ariel Pink as Holy Shit? If you were at that magnificent Ariel Pink gig nearly two years ago and loved it (of course you did) then you will love John Maus. Please check out his amazing music here:http://www.myspace.com/johnmausAnd a few excellent videos: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MpEETGLT6Vohttp://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zLVpZme0kRQhttp://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sV3AJViQWJs
