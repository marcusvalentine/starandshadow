A Little Lost Special

10:00 p.m. Friday 28 to 4:00 a.m. Saturday 29 May 2010

Start the Bank holiday weekend in proper style with a little lost and friends! We are really excited about this next party, not only is P-Skillz playing again after his brilliant set at the last night in March, but we also have DJ BoogieniteDJ Boogienite is a member of the Chicago based Chuck Bros, a collective of DJ's and Producers which includes Rahaan - who's probably the best known member to people in the UK. His sets feature rare, soulful, underground disco treats & exclusive never heard before edits. Boogienite cut his teeth as a DJ playing on the underground Chicago party scene playing alongside a wealth of DJ talent in the city which included people like Sadar. He has won the annual DJ competition on Bring The Heat website for the last two years running, taking on competitors from all round the world in a competition where you can only play rare disco records. He is also a producer releasing records under the name Urban Legendz. This is a rare appearance in the UK & is not to be missed.More on Boogienite check :http://www.bringtheheat.com/bthforum/forum/http://www.chuckbrothers.com/Mix here: http://www.zshare.net/audio/50772650bfee42d5/P. Skillz has recently came back from playing several dates in Chicago alongside people such as Rahaan & Sean Sounds who runs Bring The Heat which is one of the best websites where you can hear underground disco DJ's. He also puts on his own night Rare Funkshun infrequently, the last one featured Al Kent (Million Dollar Orchestra, BBE records) & Boogienite (Chuck Bros, Chicago). Playing mainly music from the 70's & 80's, expect to hear a set featuring exclusive edits & less know music from deep disco, afro funk, gospel, boogie & modern soul.For P-Skillz blog and some great mixes - http://givethedjabreak.blogspot.com/a little lost favourite Pat, from the infamous and legendary Road to Rimini night, will start proceedings. Be sure not to miss it!Friday the 28th May,10:30 - 04:00Star and Shadow CinemaStepney BankNE1 2NP£5Hope to see you there!To find out about more A Little Lost parties in the future please join this group:http://www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=102640736684&ref=ts
