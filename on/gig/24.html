LIVE!

Some of the most talented folk and gypsyjazz musicians in Newcastle gathered in one single night! A night to remember and to make you dance and smile…

Kate Young

'Singer songwriter Kate Young is quickly establishing a reputation in Newcastle as a fearless musician who isn't afraid of pushing any boundaries in the writing of her epic swirling songs. Utilizing a range of instruments blended with her exceptional voice Kate is going along way'

Her voice is amazing and her talent endless.
