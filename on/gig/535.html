Red Letters Acoustic, Georgia Daniels, Ryan Bird + Special Guests

8:00 p.m. to 11:00 p.m. Saturday 20 April 2013

Letters, Georgia Daniels, Ryan Bird and secret special guests. All local artists looking to please your ears and minds with their unique sounds.

Artists

Red Letters

Red Letters are a 4 piece rock/pop band situated in the North East. They’re four mates out to have fun and produce excellent music. Bound to get you up o your feet with their catchy melodies and energetic performance! Not to be missed.

http://soundcloud.com/red-letters

http://www.youtube.com/user/Redletterstv

Georgia Daniels

"You're one of the very few singers where image, voice and song material come together in one beautiful and commercial package." - Yak Bondy, Songwriter 

Singer songwriter from Houghton-le-Spring. Georgia’s set is complied of heartfelt original songs and covers that she loves. Honest, thought-provoking songs are her main aim, and her influences range from Nirvana to Agnes Obel.

www.georgiadaniels.co.uk

https://soundcloud.com/georgiadaniels

Ryan Bird

“I write songs and sing them. I swear I’m not as miserable as I seem!” Ryan’s an 18-year-old lad from Gateshead, where he spends most of his time writing and recording. New to the gigging scene, he has a lot to give and easily captivates the audience with his intimate, well-crafted songs.

http://ryanbird.bandcamp.com

https://soundcloud.com/ryan-bird
