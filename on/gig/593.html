PLEASE

(Upset The Rhythm / London / Leeds)

AGOP faves Please return to geordie shores! Nightschool Rekkids boss Michael Kasparis (also trading solo as Apostile) re-unites with Shakeeb Abu Hamdan (Cleckhuddersfax / Isambard Kingston Brunel) and Rowland Thomas (Ye Lampreys) to remind us why we started putting on shows in the first place. Duelling guitars and galloping drums make for some very finely wound sludge as Please race 60s garage and wrong-footed prog through an obstacle course, delivered with the sort of life affirming, heady rush that you'd expect of punk. YES!
