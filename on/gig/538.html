Peculiar Disco Moves

8:00 p.m. Friday 12 to 1:00 a.m. Saturday 13 April 2013

Peculiar Disco Moves - 'The Domino Effect' EP Launch GigPeculiar Disco Moves are ecstatic to present their 3-track extravaganza ‘The Domino Effect’, marked with a launch gig on Friday 12th April 2013 at The Star And Shadow.  
From humble beginnings rehearsing in a Glue Factory, an old Dentist Surgery, and a studio once used by The Lighthouse Family (until it flooded), Peculiar Disco Moves have evolved into a band that glorifies the trials and tribulations of raw British life within the boundaries of their own unique brand of feelgood, fun, inspiring progressive-pop narratives.
Tickets/General Admission £6 (available to pre-purchase)
Ticket and CD offer £8.50 (available on the door only)
Tickets are available to purchase by visiting the band's Bandcamp site
The EP is supported by a limited edition half pint glass which comes with a download code for the band’s Bandcamp page, which will be available to purchase for £4 at the launch. 
The EP will also be available to purchase in CD format also priced at £4. T
o get in there early and pre-order a copy of the EP prior to the release visit the band's bandcamp page, where you can also listen to the lead track from the EP 'Chris'.
