Nite Jewel

8:00 p.m. Wednesday 30 September to 12:00 a.m. Thursday 1 October 2009

Ramona Gonzalez band Nite Jewel is 'dusty' electronic music, home recorded on an 8 track using analogue synths to create a warm, hazy sound that broadcasts on the 'searching for happiness and meaning' band of the emotional/psychological spectrum.

With the aid of her multitrack cassette recorder and whatever keyboards, drum machines, and other instruments that were available to her, she very quickly developed her own unique sound and began performing live in the L.A. area under the moniker Nite Jewel.

A fan of ambient avant-garde electronic music as much as 90's R&B, her music reverberates between various styles guided by the technology available to her. The quality of her chosen medium seems to be neither a means nor an end, but rather a device to lend an ethereal kind of efficacy to the golden age of alternative disco.
