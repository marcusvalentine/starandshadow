Event: Groundhog Days - A Performance By David Foggo

2 Feb 2013, 7 p.m.

From the 2nd February 2008 until 14th November 2012 David Foggo collected 366 Groundhog Day vhs videos - one for each day of the year plus another for the leap year. The first fruits of this collection will be a special anniversary Groundhog Day domino rally. The performance takes place in the gallery/foyer space. An exhibition of text works and various paraphernalia inspired by the film will also be on display.
