Event: A Home For Everyone - Exhibition

10 Dec 2013, 2 p.m.

Gig: Chris T-T With Dave Hughes

10 Dec 2013, 8 p.m.

Emerging from roots in late 90s lo-fi and psych-folk, Chris-TT first gained acclaim for sarcastic, city-obsessed urban folk. Now crossing barriers between punk, psych-pop and English folk he has inspired a generation of successful home-grown artists. Joined tonight by Dave Hughes a riotus Glaswegian singer of thought provoking social-political folk songs.
