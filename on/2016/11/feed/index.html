We aim to provide a network to share experiences, resources, skills and build communities of belief, support and action. We recently launched a report-zine and toolkit to help develop knowledge and understanding about gendered violence in activist communities. We are very happy to facilitate a series of free one-day workshops funded by the Feminist Review Trust across the UK: The workshops start at 10am and finish at 5:30pm at the latest. Food and refreshments will be provided. Activists of all genders are welcome. Places are on a first come first served basis however if the workshops are over-subscribed priority will be given to activists with disciplinary, complaints, safer spaces, accountability and policy responsibilities.
